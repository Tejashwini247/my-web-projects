Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients :

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 2-3 slices of tomato
  • 2-3 slices of cucumber
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1-2 slices of cooked chicken
  • Butter or Mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps :

  1. Take 2 slices of bread and spread butter or Mayonnaise on the side of each slice.
  2. Place 1-2 lettuce leaves on one slice, then add tomato and cucumber slices.
  3. Add cheese, chicken on top of the vegetables.
  4. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Place the second slice of bread on top, buttered side down.
  6. Cut the sandwich diagonally or in half and serve.
